Biography

Christopher Edward Nolan is a British-American filmmaker. Known for his Hollywood blockbusters with complex storytelling, Nolan is considered one of the most influential filmmakers of his generation. His films have grossed more than $5 billion worldwide. He is known for his distinctive non-linear storytelling and atmospheric visual style, and his work spans genres from psychological thriller to science fiction and historical epic.
